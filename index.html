“Yapok!” Or “YPK” (Pronounced: eye-pay-ka), is a collective founded by “Niels” and “Milan” (2018) from a group of close friends. Using a whitelist, they tried to keep themselves apart from unknown outsiders. This cult-like way of operating made a Yapok what it is today.

What makes Yapok stand apart can be heard in the music, combining everything from jazz rock -to hard hitting trap with the signature electronic processed sound of the vocals.

We're not bound by the rules of art, simply because it has none.
